Hi! I'm Gregory Smith, a student at Western Michigan University studying Data Science with a minor in Computer Science. I started off at Western as a Computer Science major with no programming knowledge or experience.

Once I started coding, I was hooked. I'm an innate problem-solver, and I like to be given a challenge. So, there's no surprise that Computer Science and I were a good fit. While learning to code, I quickly realized that I preferred playing with data, whether I'm cleaning it, manipulating it, visualizing it, or drawing insights from it. Around my 2nd semester at Western, I realized that Western offered a Data Science degree. Imagine that: a major that is specialized for tackling complex problems and playing with data. I switched my major to Data Science, joined the Data Science & Statistics Club here at Western, and haven't looked back since.

I believe that there's a story waiting to be read in every dataset, and I want to be the storyteller. My story has just started, and I'm excited to see where it goes.
